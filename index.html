Game Tutorial

Back Read is composed by multiple-choice questions.

Read the reverse-text on the board and answer the question as faster as you can.

Keep an eye on the clock, the game is over when timer reaches zero.

Answering a question correctly gives you some extra time.

While a wrong answer penalizes you reducing your remaining time.

The highest your score, the harder the challenges get!
